About Your Stay

Check-in/check-out times

On the happy day you arrive check in is from 2pm. On arrival please park your car accessed via the first gate on your right off the main driveway. If for whatever reason you are held up or are arriving late, please give us a call to let us know when we can expect you.

On the sad day you have to leave South Combe Waters (for now!) please be kind enough to be vacated from your lodge at 10am. Ensuring all bed linen is stripped from the beds and left in a pillow case by the beds.

Car Parking

There is ample parking at South Combe Waters. Dormouse and Nuthatch lodges have their own parking for 2 cars. Kingfisher and Badgers Retreats' parking is approximately 20 metres away from then lodges.

Fishing

Each lodge has its very own outside tackle store which is secured via a padlock. Please feel free to use these during your stay.

Walking

A public footpath runs alongside South Combe Waters which gives access to the stunning countryside. Equally who can deny the beauty of Dartmoor to the West and Exmoor which are only a short drive away. A walkers paradise.
